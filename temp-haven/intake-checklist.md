# Intake Checklist

Questions to ask myself when evaluating a delegation request.

---

## Essential

- What is the desired outcome?
- What does success look like?
- What context does the mind need to do this?

## Helpful

- What role fits this work? Do we need a new one?
- Where does the work happen?
- How will completion be verified?
- Are there patterns or examples to reference?

---

## If Unclear

State what's missing and ask.
